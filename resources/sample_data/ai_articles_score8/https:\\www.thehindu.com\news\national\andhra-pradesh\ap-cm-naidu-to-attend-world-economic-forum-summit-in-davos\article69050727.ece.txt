
A.P. CM Naidu to attend World Economic Forum Summit in Davos


                    Chief Minister N. Chandrababu Naidu 
                                      
A delegation from Andhra Pradesh led by Chief Minister N. Chandrababu Naidu will attend the four-day World Economic Forum 2025 in Davos, Switzerland, beginning January 20. 
The State government has approved the necessary proposals for the delegation comprising Mr. Naidu, IT Minister N. Lokesh, Industries Minister T.G. Bharat, and the officials of the Industries Department and Andhra Pradesh Economic Development Board. 
During the summit, Mr. Naidu and Mr. Lokesh would highlight the resources and investment opportunities in Andhra Pradesh. 
The Davos visit will focus on highlighting the opportunities in renewable energy production, smart cities, and infrastructure projects in the State. The government will also organise an exhibition in Davos with the theme ‘Shaping the Intelligent Age’.
